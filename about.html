About MaxHealth Hospital

About Us

Maxhealth Hospitals has championed numerous social initiatives – to cite a few which assist underprivileged children – SACHi (Save a Child’s Heart Initiative) which screens and provides paediatric cardiac care for congenital heart diseases, SAHI (Society to Aid the Hearing Impaired) and the CURE Foundation focused on cancer care. To introduce population health into the Indian narrative, the Total Health Foundation, as envisaged by Dr. Reddy, is piloting a unique model of healthcare in the Thavanampalle Mandal of Andhra Pradesh. It aims to provide “holistic healthcare” for the entire community starting from birth, through one’s journey into childhood, adolescence, adulthood and old age.Welcome to MaxHealth Hospital, where your well-being is our top priority. We are dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare services with a strong focus on patient well-being. Our team of skilled professionals is committed to delivering personalized and compassionate care to ensure the highest standards of medical excellence.

Our Mission

At MaxHealth Hospital, our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare services that enhance the well-being of our community. We strive to deliver compassionate and personalized care, fostering a healthy and supportive environment for our patients.

Values
